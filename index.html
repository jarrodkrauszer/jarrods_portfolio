About
Me

My passion for coding began at a young age, where I found myself typing "goto" statements on my Commodore 64kb computer. Yes, that's right, just 64kb of memory! Fast forward 15 years, and I proudly graduated from Mercer County Community College with a degree in Applied Sciences, earning a spot on the Dean's list. Following graduation, I embarked on a career at Burlington Coat Factory, working in the Quality Assurance department. In this role, I was responsible for meticulously testing new programs before they were implemented into production. After a few years, I felt the need for a change and decided to move to Colorado, where I entered the exciting world of home automation. For the next 18 years, I specialized in programming residential and commercial control systems, ultimately achieving the prestigious title of Master Certified Crestron Programmer. This career allowed me to travel the world and work on extraordinary homes. However, with advancements in technology and the industry, I have decided to update my skill set and transition into the field of Fullstack Web Development.
