About
Me

My passion for coding started as a young boy typing goto statements on my Commadore 64kb. Yes 64kb, let that sync in for a minute!! Fast forward 15 years later I graduated on the Dean's list at Mercer County Community College with a degree in Applied Sciences. After graduation I got a job Burlington Coat Factory in the Quality Assurance department. There I was tasked with testing all the new programs before there where to be put in to production. After a few years of that I decided I needed a change. I moved out to Colorado and got in the wonderful world of home automation. I spent the next 18 years programming residential/commercial control systems and became a Master Certified Crestron Programmer. Got to travel the world and work on some of the most amazing homes/buildings. With changes in the industry allowing for newer technologies to be used it I have decided to update my skill set and become a Fullstack Web Developer.
